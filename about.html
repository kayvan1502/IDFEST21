This year IDFest is expanding its scope to include online competition along with the previously known event - KASTARA. IDFest'21 will include two (2) new categories to compete in. In addition to the competition in Mobile Legends, this year the committee presents a Minecraft building competition as well as a photography competition.

Both categories were added in line with our goal to peak interest to even younger audiences regarding cultural preservation and their role to take active part in their community. The competitions will be closed with a main event day, consisting of a Webinar, Workshop, as well as live broadcast with special music performances. These events will be inspired by Indonesia's 80s pop culture, which we hope will help reintroduce the era to the newer generation today.
